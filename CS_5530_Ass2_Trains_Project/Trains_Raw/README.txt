
The dataset consists of information about various cars, including their names, locations, years of manufacture, kilometers driven, fuel types, transmission types, owner types, mileage, engine specifications, power, seating capacity, new prices, and prices. Each row represents a different car entry, with details such as the car's make and model, the city it is located in, the year it was manufactured, the distance it has been driven, the type of fuel it uses, the transmission type, the owner type, the mileage in kilometers per liter or kilogram, engine capacity in cubic centimeters (cc), power in brake horsepower (bhp), seating capacity, the new price in lakh (if available), and the current price. This metadata provides a comprehensive overview of the dataset's contents, allowing for detailed analysis of the car-related information provided.
